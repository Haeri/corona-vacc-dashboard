Covid19 Statistics of Fully Vaccinated People in Switzerland

This page shows statistics regarding the cases, hospitalizations, and deaths of fully vaccinated people in Switzerland. The data is sourced from opendata.swiss. The source code is available on GitHub.

The calculation is done for the weekly numbers and then averaged. The efficacy is calculated with the following formula: show

vacc_num: number of incidents with vaccinated people
vacc_pop: number of vaccinated people in the population
unvacc_num: number of incidents with unvaccinated people
unvacc_pop: number of unvaccinated people in the population

(1 - (vacc_num / vacc_pop × 100) / (unvacc_num / unvacc_pop × 100)) × 100)
